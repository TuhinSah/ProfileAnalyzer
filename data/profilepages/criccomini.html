  • Senior Data Scientist

    Worked on LinkedIn's "People You May Know" algorithm. Able to improve run-time from 4-6 weeks to ~1 day (using Hadoop, Pig, and Voldemort) while simultaneously improving precision and stability. This work led to massive increase in invitations.

    Acted as engineering lead on a scalable offline/online analytic service. Wrote specification, Java code, and delivered finished service to production. Service is currently deployed in production, handling high query load with no significant performance degradation, and powering numerous LinkedIn products. Also worked cross functionally with numerous engineering teams to help them leverage the service for their own products.

    Delivered next generation "Who's Viewed My Profile" product. Work led to significant gains in revenue for LinkedIn. Worked on product in an engineering capacity as an Engineering lead to write back-end Hadoop/Pig jobs, mid-tier Java services, and front-end JSP. Acted as interim product manager. Performed 2011 planning, resource asks, design reviews, product strategy planning, and numerous presentations.

    Acted as first test user to improve LinkedIn's tech stack by working with Tools team to test and deploy new build system (Ant/Ivy/Maven/Gradle) and RPC (Protocol Buffer RPC) frameworks.

    Worked with Aster Data as one of the first users at LinkedIn to test and deploy initial Aster Data cluster.

    Performed ~200 interviews in 2 years. Helped build out Product Analytics, Search/Network/Analytics, Business Analytics, and Product Manager teams.

Samza then routes messages between stream processing tasks and the publish-subscribe systems that the messages are addressed to.
