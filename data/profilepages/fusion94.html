Summary

I'm a big believer in using tools and process to address the historic gulf between developers and operations. This is more than just software deployment; it's a whole new way of thinking about cooperation and coordination between these two groups. Areas like automation, monitoring, capacity planning & performance, backup & recovery, security, networking and provisioning can all benefit from using this model to enhance the nature and quality of interactions between development and operations teams.


I have over 21 years of professional experience in both systems and software engineering and integration (including: heterogeneous system and network design, evaluation, installation and configuration, administration and troubleshooting); expertise in data communications, mobile, search and social network development; ability to master new technologies quickly, and a proven track record of supervising and completing projects on time, on budget and with minimal supervision.


Operating Systems: FreeBSD, Linux, Mac OS X, Solaris


Programming Languages: C, Ruby, PHP, Objective-C, Javascript, HTML, SQL, XML, shell scripting.
