Summary

I manage software teams to understand things that matter, then to build tools to make them better. Sometimes I get paid, sometimes I'll do it for free. My projects have included:

Understanding:
* How non-profit organizations find and activate supporters.
* How the two sides of the brain see differently
* How, when, and why the two sides of the brain communicate.

Helping:
* Voters know who is receiving campaign funding from whom.
* Users with limited / no Internet access to leverage free digital learning resources (e.g. Khan Academy).
* Neuroscientists access and process publicly available datasets

I've used a number of technologies to do this:
* Backend: Python (Django, Flask), PHP (Laravel)
* Frontend: Javascript (Angular), CSS(LESS), HTML
* Data / Analytics / Data Science: SQL (MySQL, RedShift), MongoDB, Python (Pandas / Sklearn / TensorFlow), MATLAB (neural nets)

Patents

  • Spiking Neuron Network Apparatus and Methods for Optical Flow Object Detection Via Self-Motion Cancellation

    United States US9193075

    Optical flow for a moving platform may be encoded into pulse output. Optical flow contribution induced due to the platform self-motion may be cancelled. The cancellation may be effectuated by (i) encoding the platform motion into pulse output; and (ii) inhibiting pulse generation by neurons configured to encode optical flow component optical flow that occur based on self-motion. The motion encoded may be coupled to the optical flow encoder via one or more connections. Connection propagation delay may be configured during encoder calibration in the absence of obstacles so as to provide system specific delay matrix. The inhibition may be based on a coincident arrival of the motion spiking signal via the calibrated connections to the optical flow encoder neurons. The coincident motion pulse arrival may be utilized in order to implement an addition of two or more vector properties.

    Inventors:
    • Dr. Ben Cipollini,
    • Eugene Izhikevich,
    • Borja Ibarez
