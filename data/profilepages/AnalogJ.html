Projects

  • QuietThyme

    Starting 2012

    QuietThyme is a Cloud based ebook manager. Our goal is to provide you with the ability to access your ebook library anytime, anywhere from any device. The unfortunately the ebook ecosystem is currently fragmented by device. All of the popular ebook readers have an associated ebook store, which holds onto your purchased books as tightly as possible inside their walled garden. QuietThyme was built to tear down these unnecessary walls. Once you upload your books, we provide you with a centralized cloud storage bucket that you can access from any device without any restrictions.

    QuietThyme was built with the following core tenants: 1. Provide a simple uploading process that anyone (even the technologically inept) could follow 2. Support the two most popular ebook formats, Mobi (used with Amazon Kindles) and ePub (almost everyone else) 3. Ensure access to your library is available 24x7 via the cloud 4. Provide a way to read your books on any device, using OPDS and Send to Kindle. 5. Create a dead simple way to identify and automatically update ebook info, including Title, Authors, Description, Cover Art, etc.

    • Victoria Mach
