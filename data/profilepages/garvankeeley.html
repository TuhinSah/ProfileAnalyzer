Summary

Currently, I am the lead iOS Engineer at Brave, founded by Brendan Eich (Mozilla co-founder and author of JavaScript), building an open-source privacy-focused web browser with a novel payment model for publishers.
A graduate of U of T Computer Science, I started my career building airborne survey navigation systems at Optech, then spent nearly a decade leading development teams at Avenza Systems, building the popular Android and iOS app PDF Maps, as well as web app backends for these apps.
Following that, at Mozilla I was a senior engineer on their geolocation efforts on multiple platforms, and built Mozilla Stumbler for Android, a popular network geolocation scanner.
