Summary

I'm a full stack entrepreneur specializing in the real estate space. I love solving problems with technology and reducing friction within antiquated business practices. As an experienced engineer focused on ruby on rails, elixir / phoenix and iOS development, I have a passion for getting my hands dirty and bringing ideas to life.

The last two startups that I founded in the real estate space were acquired (Curb Call and Robot Workshop)

I founded Hoverboard Labs, a custom development shop in San Diego, in 2013, after serving as CTO of a real estate software company that acquired one of my previous startups, Robot Workshop.

I have a particular soft spot for trying to improve the real estate industry with technology after serving almost 10 years as a full time broker and brokerage owner.

Experience

  • CTO

    CurbCall
    January 2016 – Present (1 year)

    Rooted on the technology we created at Curb Call Technologies, this new company approaches the market with an all-star team to offer CurbCall Connect and CurbCall Protect solutions for the real estate industry.

    CC Connect is a platform to allow home-search websites and apps to intelligently route leads to agents who are available to get in touch or go on a home-tour in realtime, while the home shopper is still interacting with the site or app. It's API is simple to integrate into any platform and offers corresponding native apps for participating real estate agents to use to mark their own status as "available" or "unavailable" throughout the day.

    CC Protect aims to simply have loved ones and emergency contacts notified whenever a real estate agent goes on a showing. Contacts receive a simple text message each time a showing begins and can access a clickable link to display a realtime map of the agent's location for the duration of the showing.

    *** Sold company in Jan 2016 *** Curb Call is an app that connects buyers and buyer agents in real time. Buyer's who don't have a go-to agent, will often engage one for the first time, from outside of a listing that they want to see. As on-demand technology permeates our everyday lives, leaving a voicemail for a listing agent just won't cut it anymore. Buyers who are at the listing want the showing asap. With Curb Call, these buyers can request a nearby and available agent with the press of a single button. If your brokerage is using Curb Call, you could be the one receiving the lead.

Patents

  • Mobile application for real estate shopping

    United States

    According to certain inventive techniques, a buyer's mobile device running an application is in communication with a server to facilitate communication regarding the showing of a listed real estate property. Using the application, the buyer can check into the showing. If a location of the buyer's mobile device then exceeds a predetermined distance from the property, a feedback request is automatically sent to the mobile device from the server.

    Inventors:
    • Seth Siegler,
    • Rick Bengson

  • Property showing appointment scheduling system and method

    United States

    A method for scheduling a property showing includes receiving an electronic communication from a buyer, wherein the electronic communication from the buyer includes identification data to uniquely identify a property, and requested showing time data including a requested showing time for the property. It is determined whether confirmation by a seller is required prior to showing the property. If the confirmation by the seller is not required, a showing at the requested time is scheduled. Showing instructions for the property are retrieved and electronically communicated to the buyer. If confirmation by the seller is required, a confirmation request is sent to the seller via an electronic communication.

    Inventors:
