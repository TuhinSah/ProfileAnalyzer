Summary

I have focused most of my career on developing rich, front-end, web applications. In the past few years I have been integrating into full-stack JavaScript (Node) and focusing on clean code, pair-programming and test-driven development.

My history includes 15+ years with advanced experience using JavaScript, PHP, Flash/ActionScript, and HTML/CSS but it's all the blossoming NodeJS tools I enjoy working with the most in an environment that promotes and evolves with an Agile and/or XP foundation. I frequent local JavaScript, UX, IoT (Internet of things), Arduino and iOS meetups; I am always striving to constantly grow and fine-tune my self/team-management, programming, and communication skills.

Lastly, I am very happy with my current employer and project team.
