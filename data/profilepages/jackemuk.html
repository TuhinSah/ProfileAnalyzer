  • Senior System / Pipeline Developer

    Technicolor
    March 2016 – Present (10 months)

    Capitalizing on my pipeline development background in post production editorial, VFX, and finishing, I am bringing that depth of knowledge to the PULSE project as primarily a back end fullstack developer.

    Technicolor PULSE is changing the way film and broadcast productions are created. PULSE provides the technology necessary to manage a production: storage, network, and a versatile platform. Clients connect to PULSE from around the world with a web or mobile interface, in real time, while maintaining the security required for Hollywood production content. PULSE manages and processes all forms of production content: documents, images, audio, video, and original camera footage.

    The architecture of Technicolor PULSE is a highly available, secure, hybrid cloud, scalable computing platform with 24x7 monitoring and operations. Productions can trust Technicolor to keep their content secure and always available. PULSE provides applications that meet the key needs of production, but also provides APIs that allow clients and third party applications to partner with Technicolor to create their own solutions.
