Summary

I'm a front-end engineer currently focusing on AngularJS and TypeScript, to create end-to-end applications.

My path toward web development began with content—both writing and editing—then veered toward product management. While my technical knowledge helped me communicate effectively with developers to successfully build products, I realized I’d need to get my hands in the development process to become the versatile team player I aspired to be. That's when I decided to pour my creative energy into programming full time.

A background in writing and management helps me thrive in cross-functional teams where communication styles and priorities vary from member to member. As a genuine people person, I love collaborating with colleagues of different talents to solve problems and build new things.

With the approval of the ECMA 2015 spec and a web moving towards a components paradigm, I'm truly excited to be a part of this rapidly evolving space. I’m insatiably curious and a rapid learner, which makes me ideally suited to thrive in a field that’s evolving at lightning speed.
