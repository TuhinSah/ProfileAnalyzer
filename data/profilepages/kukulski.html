Summary

20 years experience designing and building highly interactive user interface at companies large and small.

I build application architectures to maximize the quality of interactive experience. I get "the good stuff" in the hands of designers, surfacing the capabilities of new technologies to enable rapid prototyping. This means that the system needn't trade-off between performance, capabilities and a well-crafted design.

At a certain level of performance and interactivity, something magical happens to the experience of a system, and the user's satisfaction and productivity skyrocket.
