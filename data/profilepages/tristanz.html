Summary

Economist, statistician, and entrepreneur interested how data can accelerate science, business, and development. Cofounded Sense, a modern platform for data science and big data analytics, that was acquired by Cloudera in 2016. Currently working at Cloudera on more powerful and productive tools for data scientists. Previously a development economist working with USAID and the World Bank.
