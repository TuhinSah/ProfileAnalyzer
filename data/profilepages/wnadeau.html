    I've grown so much personally during my time here, and I believe that is a testament to the company's growth, the culture of empowerment, and the consistent feedback loops we developed as a leadership team. Come work with us!

    - Plumbed a bunch of pipes for sales and marketing teams, from integrations to analytics dashboards

    People Leverage:

    - Created and ran a comprehensive new-hire onboarding process
    - Earned deep trust through consistent 1-1's and an open heart

    Product Leverage:
    - As a true product historian, we've avoided having to re-learn many of our toughest lessons through institutional memory, despite such rapid team growth.
    - Went deep to the edge of our b2b brand value proposition to evolve where it falls short and how we should reframe the customer conversation to give us room to scale liquidity.

    Ultimately I'm so glad that I decided to take a IC's focus on growth for a while, as I am learning a LOT about the complexities of aligning business growth goals with team & personal goals.
