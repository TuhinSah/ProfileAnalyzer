Summary

My family is my main motivator. They are the reason I strive to be dependable and adaptable, so I can provide for them. Integrity is important to me - I can be counted on to do what I say I will do - whether that means learning a new technology or being thrown into a brand new work environment with new coworkers.

As a leader, I believe everyone is programmed to do their best. Leading with respect and kindness helps bring out the best in people, and I supplement that with training, tools, or motivation. When I am not leading, I leave my ego at the door and contribute to my team however I can.

As a software engineer, I continually want to dive deeper into technologies and frameworks and use them to create meaningful apps that are designed to help others. If I am asked to do something more than a couple of times, I always consider a way to automate the process.
