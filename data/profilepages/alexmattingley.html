Summary

Hello, my name is Alex Mattingley and I am a full stack web developer with a love for simple designs and great relationships.

A couple of things that I am proud of:

I have led several web development projects and I have participated in countless others as a student of all things code. I have had the pleasure of working with Made by Grizzly a leading design and development firm in San Diego, CA and I have helped businesses succeed by either modifying their current websites or by building them out new ones.


I am predominantly a front-end developer and I can write clean beautiful code in Javascript, PHP, Mysql, HTML, and CSS/SASS. I have a solid understanding of Jquery, Ajax, REST, SOAP, Apache, Mamp, Git, Bitbucket, Github, the Chrome Developer tools, Sublime, and PhpStorm. I really like working with Wordpress as a CMS and I have developed custom themes as well as installed those developed by others.

I absolutely love what I do and for me coding is more than a job, its a passion. I am always looking for new clients, new relationships, and new experiences. If you love what you do, and you are looking for help with a project, let's talk!

-Alex Mattingley
