Summary

William Wnekowicz is a Developer in Residence at Kleiner Perkins, where he builds products to support portfolio companies.

He is an avid maker, who uses various media including electronics, software, brainwaves, paint, plastic and sound. Projects include Karma Controller (http://karmacontroller.com) and Color My Desk (http://colormydesk.com). More works are posted at http://willmakesthings.com/

William graduated with a degree in Business Administration from Georgetown University in Washington, DC, where he studied Management (MGMT) and Operations and Information Management (OPIM).

During his time at Georgetown, he co-founded Debatepedia.org, a non-profit project under the International Debate Education Association. He managed its software development project, which adapted MediaWiki to a logic-tree debate structure. The site had attracted over 4 million readers and editors and was later merged into Debatabase.

Lastly, he enjoys aviation and holds an FAA Private Pilot certificate. He has over 100 hours of flight time in single-engine aircraft.

Specialties: Javascript, React, Angular, Ruby, Rails, Python, WordPress
