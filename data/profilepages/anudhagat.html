Summary

Computer Science. Canvas, paint and design. That's all me.

I want to make websites that "wow" users by its functionality, simplicity, and beauty:
♦Websites that are quick to load and never leave users staring at the loading circle.
♦Websites that provide all the functionality without overwhelming the space with clutter.
♦Websites that are intuitive, beautiful, and delight users.

I have a MS in Computer Science and have worked as a Sr. Software Engineer in the bay area for 3 years. More recently, I have completed an online Front-End Web Development degree from Udacity. Besides my technical interests, I love to paint in oil, acrylics and watercolor. Front End Web Development is something I truly enjoy because it combines two things I love most: science and art. I have taken the past 15 years to serve as a full time mother of two wonderful kids and am now very excited to resume my career in CS as a web developer.
