Summary

I am a seasoned software professional with a diverse experience in a wide range of industries from financial services and semiconductors to networking and virtualization. I am equally comfortable with the Dev and Ops aspects of running a business. My passion lies in building simple-to-use, simple-to-maintain, self-reliant, and resilient systems that solve critical everyday problems. Usable trumps pretty, maintainable trumps clever, resilient trumps efficient.

I believe in hiring smart, passionate and growth-oriented people, identifying their unique strengths and helping them develop further, and getting the hell out of their way.
