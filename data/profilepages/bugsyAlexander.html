Summary

Hello world! I'm a designer who knows how to program... Or better yet a programmer that fixates over well-crafted products. Having a background in programming has made me a better designer and having a background in Internet marketing has made me a better developer, which are things I would have never thought would work in conjunction, but I'm glad I chose the path I'm on now.

Being an autodidact developer has been a real challenge! But I'm truly competitive by nature so I’ve had my fair share of ups and downs, which has made me more agile and adaptable. I like to think of myself as a great problem solver. But in all honesty I think I just have the patience to see a problem through. I'm also a JavaScript advocate because I happen to love its quirkiness, loose type style, and because its fun to love something that is so misunderstood/misused!

I've always been drawn to the web but not in the way of just consuming content. I want to produce products that people want to use and/or build products to simplify their day-to-day tasks. I'm currently transitioning into a MEAN Stack developer and I'm aware that I have along journey ahead me... But if you're stuck with a program, design, or want to collaborate on creating something meaningful (looking right at you startups!) feel free to contact me!
