Summary

I am a motivated, driven, web developer and father. I love to create solutions to development challenges for web applications based in (or requiring) node.js, nosql data structures, and/or cloud deployments. I am a Bachelor of Science Graduate (Full Sail University - Web Design & Development, 2011 Valedictorian), among many other things.
