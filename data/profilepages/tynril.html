Summary

Ever since I can remember, I have been a passionate self-taught programmer, starting with a Tetris remake on DOS when I was 9 years old. Since, I’ve never stopped learning, dreaming and developing.

I'm currently an R&D Engineer at Riot Games, working on adding that "s" at "Games". I was previously working at ArenaNet on Guild Wars 2, where I was a Server Programmer, with an interest in social systems. Before that, I was Technical Director at Ubisoft, working on O.zen - a game meant to teach you how to deal with stress, and Ankama, as the Client-side Project Leader on the MMORPG DOFUS and its revamp, DOFUS 2.0. I am a fast learner, and work with a variety of technologies and designs: client-server programming, databases, networks engineering, peer-to-peer applications, and a large range of OOP and non-OOP languages: C/C++, Python, PHP, SQL, ECMAScript, Java, C#, ...

My interests also go beyond the boundaries of programming. I'm very interested in game design, level design, in-game social interactions and community management. I'm passionate about what I do and a quick-learner in various fields of the gaming industry and software programming.

Specialties: Client-server software and architecture, programming and optimization of game software, graphical programming, ergonomic interface design, isometric game programming and object-oriented programming.
