Projects

  • Hackscrapers SF

    The Hackscrapers SF web app encourages San Francisco residents to get to know the skyline of their city and explore the tall buildings around them. Users can interact with sortable Bootstrap tables, a D3.js dendrogram of buildings and their respective tenants, and a Mapbox GL web map that displays markers to represent each building entry in a PostgreSQL database. When clicked, markers submit an AJAX request that returns building-specific data to display, including a Chart.js bar chart and a Flickr photo selected at random from metadata stored in a MongoDB database. The photo results can be further queried to allow users to contribute their own search terms and filter per their interests. Users can collect cards to show favorite buildings on their dashboard. SkyscraperGO! Collect all the tall buildings.
