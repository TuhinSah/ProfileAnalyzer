Summary

I'm a software engineer, entrepreneur, team leader, and occasional mentor.

Software Engineer
As the CTO of MoveMeFit, I spend most of my day writing code for our web and mobile platforms. For our mobile app, we use swift for the front-end, and ruby on rails for the API backend. For our web application, we use a mix of haml, sass, jquery, and angular for the front-end, and the same ruby on rails / postgres core for the backend. Our architecture is primarily handled via heroku.

Entrepreneur
As the Co-Founder of MoveMeFit, I've been involved with pushing the application to multiple platforms. When we expanded from web-only to web and mobile, I recruited and hired our engineering team. Based on user feedback, we made several changes to our mobile app that resulted in being featured in Apple's AppStore under both Best New Apps and Health & Fitness.


Team Leader
At MoveMeFit and at Kabam, I've led engineering teams of various sizes. Under my engineering leadership at Kabam, I transformed a dysfunctional team on a core franchise into a cohesive unit with a strong culture. During this period, we shortened our release cycle, delivered record-breaking features, and reversed declining revenue trends.

Mentor
I've served as a hiring manager at both MoveMeFit (where I recruited and hired the entire engineering team) and Kabam. I've hired both junior and senior engineers and have helped both groups grow their skill set and become strong leaders and individual contributors.

I'm most comfortable leading engineering teams and programming with ruby, ruby on rails, mysql, postgres, and javascript. I'm currently learning and improving my skills with swift, elixir, and react.

  • Using crowd-sourcing to verify the accuracy of results of client-side determinations

    United States 8852000
    Issued October 2014

    The accuracy of results of client-side determinations used for defining views of a virtual space may be verified via crowd-sourcing. Results of primary client-side determinations may be received from client computing platforms. Individual ones of the primary client-side determinations may be performed by individual client computing platforms presenting views of an instance of the virtual space to users. The results of the primary client-side determinations may form a basis for determining view information. The accuracy of the results of the primary client-side determinations may be verified by comparing the results of the primary client-side determinations with results of secondary client-side determinations performed by one or more client computing platforms. Individual ones of the secondary client-side determinations may simulate corresponding primary client-side determinations.
