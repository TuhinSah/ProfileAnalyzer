Summary

I am a game and interactive software engineer with a proven track-record designing and developing games and RIAs for web and mobile. I work mostly on the front-end but can work across the full-stack as required. I practice and advocate test-driven development and find agile environments the most productive.

As a team lead, I have experience taking on a mentoring role. I enjoy using pair-programming, code review and the tasking process to encourage better code design.

I am expert with JavaScript (HTML5, Node), ActionScript and haXe; proficient with Unity3D (C#), Ruby, Java, Python, PHP, Illustrator, Photoshop, MySQL; familiar with C++ and Objective-C.

Over the years I have specialized in inversion of control architecture, component frameworks, game engine development and client-side architecture. I have built particle systems, avatar engines, isometric worlds, 2D platformers, and 3D engines.

The potential for games to improve and enrich education is of immense interest to me. The internet has transformative potential in the field of teaching and learning that has not yet been fully understood or explored.

I enjoy public speaking and evangelizing good technology. I have presented talks at meetup groups in the UK and in the Bay Area on several occasions speaking about software architecture, and tooling for engineering.
