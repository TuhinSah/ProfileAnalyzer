Summary

Travis is an experienced product designer skilled in understanding, communicating, and solving broad and complex problems. He’s a high performing team leader working in environments where he continues to solve hard problems across a broad product scope.

Before Facebook, Travis had several years of experience working in cross functional and fast-paced startup environments solving complex business problems. Since joining Facebook, he has worked on Publisher focused products from growing the $2bn Audience Network to the formation of the nascent Publisher Solutions team. Understanding the needs of publishers and content creators, especially with respect to ad tech and monetization, is Travis' specialty.

He has extensive experience with leading design sprints, mentoring new designers, and is a passionate follower of consumer and business products and technology.
