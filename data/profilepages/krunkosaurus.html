Summary

Hi, I'm Mauvis. I'm a hands-on, full-stack CTO and technology researcher specializing in modern, responsive web applications, new technologies, and scaling on virtualized cloud environments. My love is to build it, make it scale, and make it beautiful

As a life long learner, I have spent over 10 years honing my skills in the tech industry from design and development, to web performance and security, to consulting and team management. I love to learn and teach what I've learned to others. Beyond mastering the technical aspects of my job, my interests lie in rapid prototyping, user testing, hiring star engineers, and improving products through meaningful analytics. I am a supporter of both Scrum and Lean Startup methodologies.

I have contributed to a few books as technical editor for O'Reilly and Packt Publishing (see under work history).

I used to do a bit of web security in the past and have assisted in patching / reporting exploits for many companies including Netflix, Squarespace, Foursquare, and AOL (7 web properties including their multi-site blog platform) - see LinkedIn recs for feedback from these clients.

I enjoys speaking regularly at conferences and meetups on topics such as HTML5, Backbone, Node, AWS, Lean Development, Design & User Interface, Analytics, and others.

Outside of work, I really enjoy karaoke, kayaking, and rock climbing.
