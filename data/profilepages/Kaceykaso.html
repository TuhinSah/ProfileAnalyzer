Summary

There are 2 things I love and hold dear in this world: form and function. I believe that those two things cannot exist without each other; and much like the Bauhaus taught, they should be intuitive.

My life has always been evenly balanced between my love for design and my love for programming. It is this passion that drives me, from side-projects to hackathons, to part-time classes learning a new language. In the 10 years I have been doing web design, I have never been more in love with it, or more excited for what it has become.

I have been fortunate enough to get to share and teach out that knowledge, giving tech talks and panel discussions in the office, at local schools, and tech conferences both in the USA and Canada.

I want to help make the web a better, more user-friendly place, with thoughtful design and successful user experiences.
