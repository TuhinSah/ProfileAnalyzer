Summary

Elissa joined Microsoft on December 2013 as a Technical Evangelist. Elissa speaks at Unity Porting Labs around China. She oversees plans for gaming startups and content delivery at Microsoft Game Labs. Her technology focus includes gaming technologies, development tools, cross-platform mobile development, web development, and cloud technologies.

Elissa has over 8 years of game development experience. Before joining Microsoft, Elissa has worked as the CTO of MNE Creations Ltd., a mobile educational games startup in Shanghai. Her team was selected for Microsoft AppCampus to create a language learning game for Windows Phone 8. She is experienced in graphics, AI, gameplay, physics, and UI development work. Elissa has worked at Electronic Arts, releasing games such as Fight Night 4, The Sims, Need for Speed, and more.

Elissa was born and raised in Vancouver, Canada, where she attended Simon Fraser University, and received a Bachelor's in Science degree with a major in Computing Science and a minor in Mathematics.

Her hobbies include teaching, writing, travelling, arts, and web development.
