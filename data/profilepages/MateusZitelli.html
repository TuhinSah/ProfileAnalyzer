Summary

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate student at ANU (Australian National University) in Canberra, Australia by the Brazilian governmental scholarship Science Without Borders.

The focus of my computer science degree is towards the areas of machine learning and computer vision. Currently doing academic research on the latter subject.

I also have experience in software development, mainly web development using JavaScript, with Node.js and React.js/Flux for WebApps for instance. I have some side projects with many technologies, like microcontrollers (Arduino and even AVR directly), some hacks with scientific computation (many physical simulations and some crazy automata) and much more. You are invited to check my GitHub for more projects (github.com/MateusZitell), I would be very happy to receive a pull request from you.
