  • Software Engineer & Data Scientist

    Sharethrough
    July 2011 – January 2014 (2 years 7 months)San Francisco Bay Area

    * Began as an intern in 2011 where I worked with Sharethrough's engineering team to build new features and upgrade legacy Rails 2 application to Rails 3.

    * After joining full time in early 2012, worked on the the development of the first generation of our analytics pipeline. Pipeline used Cascading on amazon EMR to digest and process gigabytes of log lines to deliver external customer facing insights and internal operations metrics.

    * Architected and implemented the second generation data pipeline using Hadoop, Scala, Scalding, and MySQL. This Pipeline is responsible for powering customer reporting, ad server models, and ad hoc analysis.

    * Worked with small team to architect and develop Sharethrough's native mobile ad server. This involved everything from writing high performance concurrent production Scala, to training and deploying accurate click models.

    * Worked with small team to architect and develop real time data processing pipeline using Apache Spark. Pipeline ingested gigabytes of data every hour for numerous internal and external product features.

    * Contributed to open source projects including Scalding, Storehaus, and the GNIP streaming library for Ruby. Gave talks at technology conferences including RubyConf and Spark Summit on topics ranging from data infrastructure, to recommendation engines.

    * Mined large amounts of user tracking data using a variety of tools including Impala, Scalding, Hive and R to extract valuable business insights.

    * Worked on core IP strategy and filed several patent applications to protect core intellectual property.

    Tools: Scala, Scalding, Hadoop, Ruby, Rails, Java, Hive, R, SQL, Machine Learning, Javascript, TDD
