Summary

As a software engineer, I have a passion for automation, scaling, and efficiency. As a founder of 2 companies, I focus on the user and bias towards action. Together, they have lead to multiple successes, including being made an AWS Community Hero, and winning a NetflixOSS Cloud Prize.

Most recently, I founded a company called CloudNative. We create a set of tools that make following AWS best practices easy. Bakery gives you automated and reliable image creation, while Delta focuses on the auto-scaling deployment model Netflix have been using for years. Together, they give you a cloud native continuous deployment pipeline. Stay tuned - there are many more tools coming.

Answers for AWS, my consulting company, focuses on helping people learn how to use Amazon Web Services and get the most out of the cloud.

Amazon have made it much easier to start a new company by lowering the costs and the amount of work required to build a new service. However, AWS is now over 40 different services, each with 100s of features. I have been fortunate enough to use Amazon Web Services over my last 4 companies (spanning 6 years), and so each new feature has been incremental to me.
During that time, I have learned what it takes to handle exponential growth, the intricacies of mobile development, and how to build and lead engineering teams.

I believe I can help startups by short-cutting their learning process, and getting them on the right track much sooner than if they did it alone.

Going back in time, I was first employee at Motally (acquired by Nokia), and second employee at two other startups (Bluepulse & AdMobius). I enjoy the rapid progress and quick decisions that come with a small yet talented team.

If you are looking for a fast-pace, dedicated and humble person to help your team, just send me a message.
