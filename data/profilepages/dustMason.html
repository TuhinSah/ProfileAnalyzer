  • Co-Founder

    Specify
    February 2010 – Present (6 years 11 months)San Francisco Bay Area

    Specify is a full-featured project management suite for interior designers and purchasers. I lead the design and development of the product, overseeing the implementation of several technologies including an XMPP messaging layer, a REST API and a native Mac OS app wrapper. Specify was profitable from day one thanks in part to our rapid development cycle and strong experience in the field of design purchasing and procurement.

  • Web Designer / Developer

    sfgreenclean.com
    January 2008 – January 2009 (1 year 1 month)

    SFGreenClean wanted to offer their dry cleaning customers an online experience which lived up to their stellar reputation for customer service. Working closely with a small team, I helped redesign their site to take advantage of systems in their cleaning plants that allows tracking each individual piece of clothing as it moves around the facility. The result: nearly 100% customer satisfaction.

  • Web Designer

    Boxport LLC
    June 2004 – December 2005 (1 year 7 months)

    At Boxport, I worked alongside the print designers and art director to create website designs for high-end hoteliers such as W Hotels, Mandarin Oriental, Kimpton, and Hard Rock Hotel, among others. Working as a team, we developed concepts and presented them to our clients, often facing tight deadlines. My responsibilities included creating functional prototypes of design concepts, producing final front-end pages coded in HTML, CSS and JavaScript, and later implementing them as ColdFusion and PHP templates under direction from the web development team. I was required to exercise my skills in both UI design and web development.
