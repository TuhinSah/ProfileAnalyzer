Summary

I have been a System Engineer since 1997 with experience in the Internet startup, Nonprofit and Government Research sectors.

I frequently work with Docker, Linux, Apache Webserver and web-based applications.

I specialize in taking existing technical systems and redesigning them to be highly-available (HA), more robust and less error prone.

I am passionate about increasing system visibility using tools such as with Nagios, syslog & Cacti. By implementing thorough monitoring systems, it is possible to greatly increase uptime, gain better insight into what causes the problems, fix the problems leading to downtime and save thousands of hours of labor.

Specialties: Unix, Red Hat Enterprise Linux, CentOS, FreeBSD, Apache, Unix Shell Scripts, Perl, CGI, C, Java, PHP, MySQL, Organization of Technical Systems, Nagios, Splunk.

* Top 1% of users at ServerFault.com, the leading Q&A site for Sysadmins (It is like StackOverflow for Sysadmins). See my profile at http://serverfault.com/users/36178/stefan-lasiewski
