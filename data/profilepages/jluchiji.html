  • FoundOPS Routing AI

    October 2012 – February 2013

    I implemented a distributed route optimization algorithm, which is based on PAM clustering for pre-processing of spatial data, and a combination of genetic algorithms and ant optimization to generate the most optimal routes for multiple agents.     Currently the project is not in active development, but I plan to continue working on it in the near future.
