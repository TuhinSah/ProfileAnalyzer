Summary

I come from a graphic and web design background which I use to achieve a customer centric approach in my programming endeavors. Early in my career I focused on HTML/CSS and web design and from there I expanded my experience further into backend development and advanced javascript.

These days I spend most of my time doing test driven rails and javascript development but I still find time to do the occasional pixel art and interface design.

Because of my background, I love working with designers. I hate saying "no" to their ambitious ideas. I believe that programmers should figure out how to build the challenging interfaces that designers envision, rather than be limited by what is easy or convenient.
