Summary

My current experience includes iOS with Swift as well as Node.js for backend development. Since 2008 I have worked with iOS. I started with Objective-C and moved to Swift 2.0 this past year. I believe strongly in good architecture and test automation to allow for high quality software and to support a great user experience. I encourage you to browse Permission Gateway and Pullup for examples of my API and UI work on GitHub.

One of the apps I built in Swift is called Air Bubbles which has been in the App Store for several months with many updates. It was the precursor to a hardware product which will be funded with a Kickstarter project. The entire app was planned, implemented and released in a month. See: http://sstools.co/airbubbles
