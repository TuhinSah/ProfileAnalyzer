Summary

Working on Intuit Workforce (previously Playbook HR before being acquired by Intuit), helping power the on-demand economy and supporting the self-employed. We are hiring awesome javascript developers (node and angular)!

Before starting Playbook HR, I was most recently a PM at Microsoft working on search engine relevance at Bing. I graduated from Stanford University in 2012 where I majored in Computer Science. At Stanford I was president of BASES, the school's largest entrepreneurship group, and currently serve on its board of advisors. Throughout college and high school, I interned in technical roles at companies in a wide range of industries and sizes from energy to finance to social media and from small startups to fortune 500 corporations.

I'm a big data junkie and a full-stack web developer who taught himself how to program in middle school. I started my first company in high school.
