Summary

Entrepreneur, software architect, product strategist and total geek. Passionate about technology, design and creating software that is a joy to use. Founded and worked at both product firms and consultancies. Always looking for new challenges and ways to push the bounds of my knowledge and experiences.

Whether creating products, writing code or designing solutions I always place myself in the shoes of those on the receiving end of the creation. I'm fanatical about ensuring code is logical and will be understandable to the person that must maintain it 5 years from now (especially if that person is me).

In the past 15 years I've built products and solutions across myriad technologies. My career started in the heyday of “multimedia” building educational software in Macromedia Director. Lingo was the first programming language I shipped code in and I have fond memories of learning from and eventually teaching on the Direct-L listserv. Since then I've built systems with classic ASP at the peak of the dot-com boom, video conferencing software in C++/CLI with WPF, and real-time APIs powered by Node.js. With the explosion of smartphone software 5 years ago I've focused significant attention on iOS and Objective-C. It's a platform with boundless opportunity for creative expression and an opportunity to rethink decades of software product thinking.

I continue to remain excited about whatever is right around the corner.
