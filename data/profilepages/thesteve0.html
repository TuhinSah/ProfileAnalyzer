Summary

My goal is to get developers excited about working with technology and helping them to create great solutions!

I love the balance of the advocate/evangelist role - part marketing, part engineer, part product manager, part support, and part sales engineer. I consider myself a bridge between all these groups. I have broad speaking experience, from groups of 5 to halls of 300. I have taught bootcamps, workshops, and tutored other developers. Getting to work with engineers to design and test APIs, file bugs, feature improvements, and filter information back from external developers is also another great aspect to the job. I work with marketing and sales to create our value proposition and key statements to get developers to pay attention to a platform. Finally, I keep well read on trends in technology and development - it helps when talking to developers but also to set direction for our developer products.

I have expertise in Java, Python, Android, JavaScript, and HTML as languages. The main platforms I target are web and mobile applications. Some of my deepest areas of technical expertise are spatial technologies, statistics, and data analysis.
