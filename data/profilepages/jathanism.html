Summary

I have a long history with over 20 years of networking, system administration, software engineering, and security. My career has been dedicated to applying common sense security methods and industry standards to building and managing resilient technical infrastructure. I aim to make the Internet and all the systems involved not only easier to use, but easier to understand.

Specialties: System engineering, Python development, and user-friendly design. I am excellent at integrating large-scale systems for a specific task with a focus on security, and producing an interface to those systems that is useful and intuitive.

I have experience with the following: Linux, Solaris/SunOS, HP-UX, Mac OS, Windows, TCP/IP, IPsec, VPNs, BGP, IS/IS, OSPF, NetScreen, Juniper, Cisco, Foundry, CheckPoint, Python, Perl, PHP, SQL/NoSQL, HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, AJAX, XML, JSON.
