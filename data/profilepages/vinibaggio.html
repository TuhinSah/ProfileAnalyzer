Summary

I have been writing web applications since 2002, doing freelancing and small projects. In 2008 I started internship for college and my projects became much more serious. Since then, I have developed web apps using a handful of different technologies, but my specialties became Java and then Ruby on Rails. I've been involved in several different types of projects: credit card processing, e-commerces, social networks, mobile, training, capacity planning and measurement, content management systems and IT service management.

In the majority of those experiences I used Ruby on Rails as the main technology, using it since its early days, and writing professional apps with it since 2009. Because of that, I have comprehensive acknowledgement in its source code and its ecosystem.

Although I have much more experience in backend, I try to work on the full stack of web applications, such as deployment/server management and also front end development, using javascript (jQuery, backbone.js, jasmine), SCSS, SASS and HTML5 as well.

I also have great interest in developer communities, constantly talking on GURU-SP, one of the largest Ruby communities in Brazil, QCon - SP and others.

Specialties: Ruby and Rails, Javascript, Java, Linux server management, Git, agile processes (scrum and XP). I play along well with languages like Perl, Python and C.
