Summary

I am a lifelong technology lover, with multidisciplinary skills spanning across hardware, software, infrastructure and security.

Recognized innovator, I always strive to bring creative solutions to complex technical challenges, in domains of expertise as varied as content management, analytics and optimization in my role as a solution architect for the Adobe Marketing Cloud, or previously security and digital identity for large corporate and national security projects, and product development for marine Seismic acquisition systems when I started my career in the Oilfield industry.
