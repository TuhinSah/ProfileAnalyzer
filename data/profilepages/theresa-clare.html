Projects

  • Mineralchemy

    Starting May 2015

    Mineralchemy expedites collecting minerals. This app aggregates listings from Etsy, eBay and MinFind based on the user’s search specifications. Users can click on a listing's title to be redirected to the original listing, or create an account to save listings for future use. The discovery section of Mineralchemy constructs a full visualization of the classification system. Clicking on a node in each radial tree allows users to learn more about the specific details of the mineral.

    Technology Stack: Python, Flask, JavaScript (jQuery, AJAX), HTML5, CSS3, SQL, D3.js, Bootstrap.js, Etsy API, eBay API, BeautifulSoup.py
