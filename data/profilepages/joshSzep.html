Summary

A computer geek since birth, I began programming custom ZZT levels when I was 11 and have followed this passion ever since. I have been a engineering software professionally since 2006.

My passion is people and improving lives through the amazing ability to turn abstract ideas into a reality through technology. My greatest asset is my hunger for knowledge, drive and desire for continual learning, and my ability to see problems from a big picture perspective.

I thrive in environments which give me freedom to take the initiative in delivering real value, suffer in heavily managed environments which restrict me into a 'code monkey' box, and always am looking for opportunities to improve myself and the technologies I work on.

Currently focused on the bleeding edge of the rapidly merging web space, I was previously a game developer, but I have always had a generalist and "full stack" mentality. The ironic truth of the matter is I am least interested in things I have the most experience in: I love to always be learning and growing!

Some of my other interests include: music, dancing, the outdoors, motorcycles, psychology, and philosophy.
