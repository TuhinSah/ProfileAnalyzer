Summary

I am an experienced build/release engineer and source code management administrator with a developer background. I focus on helping software development teams deliver solutions at a fast pace, with high quality, and with the best tools available. I constantly look to optimize processes in all facets of software development and I enjoy building solutions to complex problems. My goal is to become a more accomplished leader through demonstrated skills, knowledge, and innovation.

I strive to automate everything where possible and I am well-versed in Jenkins because of it. I also believe that everything should be version controlled, using Git on a daily basis for code, scripts, configuration, and sometimes even documentation. I enjoy fast build times, projects that have a successful Gradle or Maven build right after cloning, and clean pull requests.

My build engineering skill set encompasses tools for the Java EE technology stack (with a focus on web applications), Android applications, and iOS applications. I have a clear understanding of the JVM and engage in Java development on a regular basis. I always consider cross-platform compatibility when introducing new development tools or automation tools. I am comfortable with Windows, Linux, and Mac OS X.

As a firm believer in open-source software, I find myself visiting GitHub almost every day and often contributing back to the projects that I use on a daily basis. When I get the chance, I hope to improve software delivery processes across the industry by open-sourcing plugins and snippets that the community may find useful.
