  • Actify

  • #SETIDecryptChallenge

    Starting May 2016

    #SETIDecryptChallenge was a call for a fun scientific challenge. A hypothetical alien signal was received in the form of pulses in a very narrow band around an electromagnetic frequency of 452.12919 MHz. The goal was to decrypt the message and be able to answer several questions about our interstellar counterparts.

    This project was a collaborative effort requiring creative thinking, analyzing/creating txt files, and writing decryption methods.

    Link to the answers and list of solvers: http://www2.mps.mpg.de/homes/heller/downloads/files/SETI_crackers.txt

    Team members:
    • Brian Hayzlett,
    • Benjamin Zagorski,
    • Ksenia Kriksunov,
    • Matthew Dubin

  • Robotic Bagpipes

    Starting October 2012

    -Final Project for Intro to Robotics Class
    -Air supplied to the bagpipes by an industrial blower controlled by a Variac transformer which was turned on and off via a 12 v auto relay
    -Finger holes on the bagpipe chanter are opened and closed by eight 5 v solenoids controlled by H bridges
    -An FPGA controls all the electronics & reads MIDI files so bagpipes can play different songs
