Summary

Paul Alexander ,LCSW-R is a Licensed Social Worker and Psychotherapist who after working in a Children's Hospice and Hospital began to integrate his music with healing grief support. Thus , griefsong.com was created and Paul shared workshops, music and inspirational forums throughout the country for the bereaved as well as helping professionals. Paul continues to offer workshops and concerts as outlined at www.griefsong.com to church groups, hospices, funeral homes and grief support networks.
His newest series of workshops are under the umbrella of Hopeful HeartSong Workshops which acknowledge the challenge of loss but focuses more on living our authentic life with purpose and direction and hopefully happiness.
As a psychotherapist, and spiritually focused on life direction, Paul is available via SKYPE for limited sessions . As an energy healer using Reiki and IET, Paul integrates these healing modalities if requested with more traditional and innovative helpful therapeutic exploration.
Paul's offers in person therapeutic sessions in Bridgehampton, NY and travels for workshop, concert and retreat programs .

Using music, and a variety of creative and helpful modalities, guided imagery, inspirational stories and strategies for self care, stress release, grief care and music therapy techniques Paul's work offers healing and hope on emotional, spiritual and psychological levels.

Goals to further help and support people with music and healing resources through music, books, dvd's, concerts and workshops.
Paul continues to offer in person psychotherapy services www.hamptonspsychotherapy.com
