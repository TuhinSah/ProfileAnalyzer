Summary

I'm an iOS engineer at Goodreads, building the official Goodreads iOS app (http://apple.co/1x2gvhz) and the api's that drive it. Previously, I worked on the web team building Ruby on Rails apps that help people share what they're reading and discover their next favorite book.

Before Goodreads, I worked on the Data Engineering team at LivingSocial, making Rails apps that interface with Hadoop and Hive for analytics and reporting.

I have a high attention to detail and enjoy building high-quality, usable applications that make people's lives easier, and interacting with the people that use them.

In addition to iOS and Ruby/Rails, I have experience with Java, Python, Javascript, and SQL, and can very quickly become comfortable with new languages and environments.
