Summary

I joined The Mobile Majority in 2014, to help build a mobile advertising and ad analytics platform. After completing the dashboard in early 2016, I moved my focus to third party integrations and am currently scrum master and UI Engineer on the what we're calling Partner Systems. My primary goals this year include continuing to expand on best practices in Angular (our framework of choice) and finding ways to integrate new paradigms like flux and redux into our codebase.

On the side, I enjoy listening to podcasts about media, politics, economics, design and history. Some of my favorites are Freakonomics, 99% Invisible and On the Media. I love a good nature walk and a relaxing Yoga session.
