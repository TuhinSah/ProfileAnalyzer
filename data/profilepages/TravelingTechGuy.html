Summary

Currently seeking a senior engineering or professional services management position, with an entrepreneurially spirited company.

(note: Please DO NOT contact me if you're trying to sell me, or my company your service. This is not what LinkedIn is about. Your connection request will just end up in my spam folder.)

I have a proven track record of designing, architecting, developing, and deploying software solutions. My last two positions as a technical co-founder/CTO allowed me to use all these skills, and turn a glimmer of an idea into a working product.

My current interest is in delivering robust web (NodeJS, PHP, JavaScript) and mobile (Android, iOS, JavaScript, WP) solutions. I have background in enterprise software development (.Net, PHP), deployment and services delivery.

I pride myself on being a quick learner. I've implemented software through all steps of the SDLC, in many platforms, environments, languages, and paradigms. My expertise is reducing any problem to its base elements, and finding the right tools to solve them.

In addition to my software delivery skills, I have extensive experience managing software, and professional services teams (including global and outsourced teams), and training engineers.

In my spare time I enjoy traveling, reading, blogging, and attempting to stay awake for 36 hours straight at mobile hackathons.
