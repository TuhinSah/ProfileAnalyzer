Summary

I’m a full stack software developer with a background in technical communications and experience building with augmented reality. As a Certified Scrum Master, I love great products and organized processes, especially diagrams, flowcharts, and visual aids. I’m part of a team that got press coverage on TechCrunch at our first hackathon for a project called Phoney.club. I’m the co-founder of Rabbit Guides, a whimsical series of travel guides for children. In my free time, you’ll find me in Marrakech, or drawing, digitally painting, and doing yoga.
