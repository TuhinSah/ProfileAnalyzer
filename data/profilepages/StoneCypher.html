Summary

I have experience in the enterprise development, private development, government contractor and hosting industries. I have some commercial gaming background, particularly in Nintendo DS, Nintendo GBA and cell phone work, but also some initial exploration into the Flash market. I have professional experience in more than a dozen langauges. Most interesting among those to companies tend these days to be C++, C, Java, Erlang, PHP, Javascript/Ecma/Actionscript, Ansi SQL, MySQL dialect SQL and CSS.

My goal is simple: galactic domination.

A great many of my accomplishments were at the job, under pressure without context. "Here, go implement this thing you've never done before." As such, I am not particularly afraid of tasks I have no idea how to do - indeed when they seem interesting, I often look forward to them.

I have worked in a number of security and safety critical fields, and have developed a deep respect for testing methodology, and for companies who employ aggressive testing staff. I believe in stochastic verification as a much more powerful tool than is commonly recognized, and I think REPL modifiers like Ruby Heckle are potentially radically powerful meta-testing tools.

I believe deeply that testing and documentation save more time than they cost, and therefore are effectively cheaper than free in situations where one is not against the wall, time-wise. I believe that it is very rare for a situation to call for diving into work without a specification and tests, though I do believe such situations exist. I am similarly a proponent of time tracking, and of data driven prediction methods (when I'm choosing, PSP/TSP/CMMI.)

I am made of magic beans.

Specialties: Software architecture, testing methodologies, embedded development, massively parallel server design strategies, and a personal interest in gaming.

I have significant experience in C/C++, PHP, Erlang, Delphi, JavaScript/Actionscript (and thus by extension other ECMA dialects), support languages such as SQL and XML, and moderate experience in dozens of other languages.
