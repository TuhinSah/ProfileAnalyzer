Summary

I'm a programming language nerd that's somehow ended up doing mostly frontend work on big fat JavaScript applications. I've got plenty of experience doing backend work, including relational and document databases, plain old dynamic-scripting-language web frameworks, weirdo distributed, robust architectures with Erlang, and big systems with Common Lisp.

I also somehow fall into the devops and git guru roles in any team I join, and I like to think of myself as a tool smith who tries her best at making sure everyone on the team has the tools and answers they need to succeed.

I'm really into Web Components and other edge tech in frontend JavaScript land lately, and can probably talk your ear off about Comp Sci stuff even though I never went to school for it. Mostly I'm just kind of enthusiastic about technical stuff.
