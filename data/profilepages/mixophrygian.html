Projects

  • Save a Stray - React Native app

    June 2016 – August 2016

    Imagine you’re traveling and come upon a stray animal. A missing dog, scared and lost. Defenseless kittens, hungry and vulnerable. What would you do? Where could you take them? Save a Stray helps you locate, call and get directions to the nearest animal rescue shelters. With three taps you can find a shelter, as well as obtain its listed phone number, address and directions from your present location. You can also center your search elsewhere using city, zip code or neighborhood name. A little time spent can mean the world to a lost animal.

    Save a Stray is a mobile app built using react-native and the yelp API. It's currently available for iOS and Android in the App Store and Google Play Store.

    Team members:
    • Eleanor Weigert

  • Flashlight Effect - HTML5

    Starting August 2016

    An on-hover flashlight effecting using three HTML5 canvas elements - one as the background image, one as the filter or "flashlight" shape, and one as the composite of the two canvases blended together manually. "But why not just use the canvas' built-in blend mode?" This project was spec work for a client that required compatibility with IE10, which the canvas' built-in blend modes do not entirely support. So, you're welcome IE users! Check out the URL for a demo or the code to try it yourself.
